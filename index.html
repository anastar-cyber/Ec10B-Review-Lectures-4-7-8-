Four Pillars of U.S. Retirement Income
PillarKey Details
1. Social SecurityEmployee + employer each pay 6.2% (cap $184,500 in 2026). Avg benefit ~$2,071/mo. Replacement rate ~90% low earners, ~28% high earners.
2. Defined Contribution (401k)Employee cap $24,500/yr. Most employers match (e.g. 50¢ per $1 up to 6% of salary). Target Date Funds common.
3. Defined Benefit (Pension)Employer pays guaranteed annuity after retirement. Rare for this generation outside U.S. public sector.
4. IRAUsed by self-employed, small-company workers, or to roll over a 401k after leaving a job.
